// About CDSC

The First Student
Community at SCOE

On 13th October 2025, a gap was noticed — the Computer Department had no structured student network. No platform for peer mentorship, no central hub for resources, no unified community identity.

CDSC@SCOE was founded to change that. A verified, multi-domain, student-driven community designed to outlast batches and establish a lasting legacy of collaboration at Siddhant College of Engineering.

Our mission is simple: Build. Share. Grow. — together, across every year and every specialization in the department.

🧠
Peer Learning
1:1 doubt sessions, daily DSA challenges, and domain-specific mentorship.
🤝
Collaboration
Cross-year, cross-domain projects and team-building opportunities.
📚
Resources
Verified notes, roadmaps, free certifications, and curated study material.
🚀
Career Growth
LinkedIn mentorship, resume building, GATE prep, and placement guidance.

// Journey

Milestones & Timeline

13 OCT 2025
Community Founded
CDSC@SCOE officially conceptualized. Official email and social handles created.
14 OCT 2025
Guidelines Finalized & Registration Launched
Community rules decided. Google registration form circulated across all department groups.
19 OCT 2025
Official Community Opening + Website Live
Invites sent personally via email. Official community website launched.
21 OCT 2025
Daily DSA Challenge Begins
Daily problem-solving sessions started — the first academic engagement in the community.
27 OCT 2025
First 1:1 DSA Doubt Session
Personalized peer mentoring on Google Meet. Marked the true spirit of CDSC — students helping students.
01 NOV 2025
LinkedIn Launch
CDSC becomes publicly visible — officially recognized as a structured student community, not just a chat group.
15 NOV 2025
First YouTube Tutorial
LaTeX Resume tutorial published. CDSC expands from text-based sharing to multimedia content delivery.
20 NOV 2025
Multi-Platform Presence Established
CDSC now active on YouTube, LinkedIn, GitHub, Twitter (X), and Instagram.
29 NOV 2025
119 Verified Members
End of Month 2. 119 registered, verified internal members — proof of sustained engagement and growth.
